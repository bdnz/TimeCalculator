 program that prompts the user for two times (as HH:MM:SS)
 displays the total number of seconds for each time, adds them together, and displays the new time in HH:MM:SS format. 

Note: new time should be display as a Clock, in a 24 hour format. 
You do not have to track the 'roll over' days.

Example output: 

Enter the first time (HH:MM:SS): 05:15:19

Enter the second time (HH:MM:SS): 06:12:45

first time in seconds:  18919      second time in seconds:  22365

New Time: 11:28:04

Again? (y/n): Y
